# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 19/03/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BitWave Studios collects and uses your data.

## Data We Collect
- Account data (email, username)
- Usage data (gameplay, logs)
- Device information (IP, OS, hardware)
- Cookies and tracking data

## How We Use Data
- Provide and maintain our services
- Improve gameplay and features
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Communicate with users

## Legal Basis (UK/EU/EEA GDPR)
We process data based on:
- Contract (providing services)
- Legitimate interests (improving services)
- Consent (marketing communications and optional features)

## Cookies
We use cookies to:
- Keep you logged in
- Analyze traffic and usage
- Improve user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

## Data Sharing
We may share data with:
- Hosting providers
- Analytics services
- Legal authorities when required

## Data Retention
We retain data only as long as necessary.

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Request deletion
- Correct inaccuracies
- Withdraw consent

## Security
We implement reasonable measures to protect your data.

## Children's Privacy
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover we have, we will delete it.
