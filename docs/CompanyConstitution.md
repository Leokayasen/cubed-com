# COMPANY CONSTITUTION – BitWave Studios

## Name
The company shall be known as BitWave Studios.

## Purpose

The purpose of the company is to:
* Develop games and software
* Publish and distribute digital products
* Support community-driven content and modding

## Structure

The company shall consist of:

* Founder(s)
* Core Team Members
* Contributors

## Roles and Responsibilities

**Founder(s):**

* Strategic direction
* Final decision-making authority

**Core Team:**

* Development, design, and operations
* Voting on major decisions

**Contributors:**

* Assist with development, art, writing, or testing

## Decision Making

* Major decisions require majority agreement among Core Team
* Founders retain veto power (optional—your choice)

## Intellectual Property

* All work created for the company belongs to the company unless otherwise agreed
* Contributors may be credited appropriately

## Revenue and Compensation

* Revenue distribution (define clearly if applicable)
* Contributors may be unpaid unless specified

## Code of Conduct

Members must:

* Act respectfully
* Avoid harassment or discrimination
* Maintain professionalism

## Amendments

This constitution may be amended by majority agreement.

## Dissolution

If the company dissolves:

* Assets will be handled according to prior agreement
